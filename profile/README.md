## About Us

We are currently a small two-person group dedicated toc creating third-party content for the tabletop roleplaying game [LANCER](https://massifpress.com/lancer) by **Massif Press**.

We are not affiliated with **Massif Press**, we just enjoy **LANCER** and have decided that we wanted to create content for other players and GMs to enjoy using in their own campaigns.
<br>
<br>
<br>
<br>

## Featured Project

### [LANCER: Reditus Company Expansion](https://github.com/DUO-R-R/LANCER-Reditus-Company-Expansion)

The **Reditus Company Expansion** offers a variety of new gear, weapons, and more for use in campaigns offered by the Reditus Technology Corporation (RTC) in addition to more general gear.

The project just recently switched from a **prototype** to **in development**! We will still actively take feedback to further improve it and develop it.
